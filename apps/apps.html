Mobile apps

Sysdevida is available on mobile devices as a Progressive Web App.

Android app
  1. Install Sysdevida from the Google Play store.

Or:

  1. On your Android device, open Chrome and go to twa.Sysdevida.com.
  2. In the menu, click 'Add to Home screen'.
iOS app
  1. On your iOS device, open Safari and go to twa.Sysdevida.com.
  2. In the Share menu, click 'Add to Home Screen'.
